Taylor Swift's cats

by Mg21

Her first cat: Meredith Grey

Taylor's first cat, Meredith, is a female Scottish Fold cat with a special place in Taylor's Heart. She was adopted by the pop-star 14 years ago, 2011. Though a lot of cats are known for hating their owners, Meredith shares the same amount of affection Taylor does for her. According to some sources, Meredith would often sing along to her "Mother"'s songs when they are played. She would also meow almost as if she was whining everytime Swift stops petting or brushing her fur. Just like her owner, Meredith also has a fanbase of her own. Her most popular appearance was in Deadpool 2, where she appeared on a character's shirt alongside her sister, Olivia, with a text below them saying, "Olivia & Meredith Best Friends Purrrrr-Ever." Though she and her mother, again, have lots of similarities, Olivia absolutely resents the spotlight. Taylor mentions this fact in a video with her youngest child, Benjamin Button, where she tells him about the "rumors" spiraling around Meredith about the fact that she hates getting her picture taken.
